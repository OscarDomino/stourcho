Patrons

Patrons are a very important part of the Society.

Starting with a minimum donation of £25, if you become a Patron you will:

  • receive one complimentary ticket for each of the main concerts with reserved seating;
  • be kept informed of forthcoming events;
  • receive an invitation to the social events;
  • have your name included with the list of Patrons in the concert programme.

To find out more, email Beate Harden (Patrons' Secretary) using our Contact form

Programme Advertising

We welcome advertisements by local organisations in our concert programmes which are produced two to three times per year. Programmes are produced in high quality full colour, and offer a great value opportunity to promote your business.

Current Advertising Rates
Whole page £50
Half page £25
Quarter page £12.50

To find out more information or to place an advert in one of our programmes please Contact us.
