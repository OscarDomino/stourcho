Vocal coach: Susie Hyman

Susie is an experienced piano and singing teacher with an extensive private teaching practice. As well as coaching the Stour Choral Society, Susie conducts three children's choirs and has been Director of Music at St Mary's, East Bergholt and St Mary's, Lawford. Susie studied singing with Norman Tattersall in Colchester and Eileen Price in London and she gives several concerts each year as a soprano soloist, as well as singing with the University of Essex Choir. Susie is a member of the concert group Con Spirito.
