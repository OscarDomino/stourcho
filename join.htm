Join Us

If you enjoy singing and think you would like to join us please come along to a rehearsal and try us out.

Rehearsals take place on Monday evenings between 7.30pm and 9.30pm at Manningtree Methodist Church Hall, South Street Manningtree, Essex. CO11 1BQ. Click here for a map.

The session starts with some warm-ups before moving on to the programme music and we take a tea break 8.30pm.

No auditions are required and we are happy to help with queries about vocal range and parts. Sight-reading ability would be helpful but this is not absolutely essential.

Subscriptions

The current annual membership fee, which supports the running costs of the Society, is £85 if paid in one instalment. Alternatively, members may make two payments of £45. There is no fee for members in full-time education.
