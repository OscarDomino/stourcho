Rehearsal Schedule 2019/20

Rehearsals are held in the Methodist Hall, South Street, Manningtree on Monday evenings, 7.30 - 9.30pm. Please remember to bring a pencil with you to rehearsals.

Members are asked to let the Voice Reps know if they are unable to attend a rehearsal.
